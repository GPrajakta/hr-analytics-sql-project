# Business Questions This Project Answers

Each question is framed as a real stakeholder would ask it — not as a technical exercise.

## Workforce Overview
1. How many people work in each department right now?
2. Is our workforce gender-balanced, or are certain departments male/female dominated?
3. Are we aging as a company? What does our workforce age profile look like?

## Attrition & Retention
4. Which departments have the highest attrition rate, and how has it changed year over year?
5. On average, how long do employees stay before leaving? Is this changing?
6. Can we identify employees likely to leave before they do?

## Salary Analytics
7. What is the average, minimum, and maximum salary by department?
8. Are we paying men and women equally for the same work?
9. Who are our top earners, and what departments/titles do they hold?
10. Are salaries growing fast enough to retain talent, or are we eroding real pay?

## Tenure & Promotion
11. How long does it take employees to receive their first promotion? Is this consistent?
12. Do we have a healthy balance of new, mid-tenure, and experienced employees?

## Management
13. How large are each manager's teams? Are any managers over-stretched?
14. Are managers paid proportionally more than their teams, or is the gap too wide/narrow?

## Hiring
15. When does the company hire the most? Are there seasonal patterns?
16. Are we paying new hires fairly compared to existing employees doing the same job?
